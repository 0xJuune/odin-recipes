Floradora

Sweet, sweet Raspberry mixed with Gin, Lime, and Ginger.

A complex but refreshingly fruity cocktail! Lovely on hot days or if you're craving sweet carbonation. Be warned: You have to love Raspberries and can't be afraid of ginger undertones. One of my all time favorite drinks.

Note: You should have Ice, a Cocktail Shaker, and something to Muddle with for this drink.

Ingredients

  • 2oz Gin
  • 1/2oz Simple Syrup
  • 2 Limes (or 3/4oz Lime Juice)
  • A small handful of Raspberries
  • Ginger Ale

Steps

  1. Start off by squeezing your limes for juice and add it to the shaker.
  2. Add your Raspberries to your shaker, and muddle them finely.
  3. Next, take the rest of your measured liquids and add them to your shaker. Save the Ginger Ale for last!
  4. Shake the ingredients heavily, double strain into glass, and serve on ice. Top off the drink with a splash of Ginger Ale.
